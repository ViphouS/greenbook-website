Fueled by the social impact and education, Green Book Project is a youth-led charity launched in April 2022, with the mission to collect, recycle, and deliver new/old books to school’s libraries in rural areas across Cambodia.

We believe that every child deserves an opportunity to explore the world at a very young age through book reading and incubate them to be creative and knowledgeable.

Our Mission

We empower rural Cambodian students by fostering a love of reading and providing access to essential learning materials. We believe every child deserves the opportunity to explore the world trough the books.

Who Matters

Young students are the future of the country. By nurturing the potential of rural youth, we cultivate the seeds of a brighter future for Cambodia.

Project Achievements

Launched in April 2022, the GreenBook Project has made significant strides through impactful campaigns. Beyond our initiatives, we've also collaborated with fellow Phnom Penh charities like AIESEC's Smiling Day and Paragon IU's Chong cham 2.0, contributing to their efforts for a wider community impact
